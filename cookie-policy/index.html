What are cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files stored on your device to remember settings, measure sessions, and support security. They can be first-party or third-party, and each type serves a specific purpose. Session cookies expire when you close your browser, while persistent cookies stay until they expire or are deleted. Some cookies are essential for core functions to load properly. Others help us analyze usage trends to improve features. You can manage them through your browser controls.
